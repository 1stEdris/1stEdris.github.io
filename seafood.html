Seafood Specialties

Discover the finest seafood delicacies from the depths of the ocean, prepared with expert craftsmanship and fresh ingredients.

Thai Fish Curry - Fish simmered in rich coconut milk with aromatic Thai curry paste.

Salmon Sushi - Vinegared rice topped with fresh salmon, often served with wasabi and soy sauce.

Ikan Goreng - Whole fish marinated with turmeric and spices, then deep-fried until crispy.

Goan Prawn Curry - Prawns cooked in a spicy and tangy coconut-based curry from Goa, India.

Mashwi Samak - Whole fish grilled with saffron, cumin and dried lime, popular in Gulf countries.

Pasta con Gamberi - Pasta with shrimp, garlic, chili flakes, and parsley in olive oil.

(Fried Squid) One of the most famous seafood dishes with a special sauce.

Fish and Chips - Battered and fried white fish fillets served with thick-cut fried potatoes.

Bouillabaisse - Traditional Provençal fish stew with various fish and shellfish, flavored with saffron.

Gambas al Pil Pil - Shrimp sizzled in clay dish with olive oil, garlic, and dried chili peppers.

Olivier Salad with Shrimp - Classic Russian salad with boiled potatoes, vegetables, and shrimp in mayonnaise.

Fish Tacos - Soft tortillas filled with grilled or fried fish, cabbage slaw, and creamy sauce.

Seafood Gumbo - Hearty stew from Louisiana with shrimp, okra, and vegetables in rich broth.

Peruvian Ceviche - Fresh raw fish cured in citrus juices, with onions, chili peppers, and cilantro.

Pastel de Camarão - Crispy fried pastry filled with seasoned ground shrimp and vegetables.
